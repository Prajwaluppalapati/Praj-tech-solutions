Best solution for slow phone / lagging phone / hanging phone

Clear Cache Data

1. Go to the settings app in your phone

2. Open the apps in the settings

3. Select any app which occupies or uses more space

4. Find "Storage" and select it

5. click on clear cache



Clear background apps

1. Tap on the square icon at the bottom of your phone



2. Clear all unnessary background apps by swiping it
upwards


Use lighter versions of apps

1. You can get lighter versions of most of the apps

2. You can get lighter version of apps like Spotify,
Facebook Instagram, Google go, Uber lite etc...
on playstore





3. You can also get light version of apps with chrome

4. Go to the app's website you want the light version of

5. Click on the three dots at the top right corner

6. Click on "Add to home screen"

7. You can get a light version by this simple method


Update software

1. Check for updates on your phone regularly

2. Go to the phone settings

3. Look for "System" or "software updates"

4. Tap on download and install, then follow the steps



Recommendations

1. Restart your phone regularly (once 2 weeks)

2. Use a static wallpaper, don't use live wallpaper

3. Don't use too many widgets

4. Don't use too many apps at once

5. Don't use too many apps which uses location

6. Use dark mode as much as you can

7. Free up storage by deleting unnecessary files, apps and photos

Best solution for App not responding / Crashing


Clear cache

1. Go to settings app on your phone

2. Open the apps in the settings

3. Select the app which is crashing

4. Find "Storage" and select it

5. click on clear cache



Check for updates


1. Go to the playstore

2. Search for the app which is crashing

3. If there is an update available, update it immediately
